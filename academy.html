TSCFG Academy

Our academy is the foundation of our club. We focus on grassroots development, providing young players with the skills and discipline needed to succeed in football and in life.

Academy Programs

Ages U12 - U15

Boys Academy

Focus on fundamental technical skills and team dynamics. Preparing athletes for high school football.

  • 2 Weekly Training Sessions
  • Local League Participation
  • Quarterly Evaluations
  • Skill-specific Drills
Ages U13 - U17

Female Academy

Building confidence and tactical intelligence in a supportive, high-performance environment.

  • Specialized Coaching
  • College Pathway Exposure
  • Agility & Speed Training
  • Team Building Workshops
Ages U16 - U19

Male Academy

Pre-professional training for athletes aiming for college recruitment or professional trials.

  • Advanced Tactical Sessions
  • Scouting & Video Analysis
  • Physical Conditioning
  • Competitive Showcase Matches
All Ages

GK Academy

Specialized training for the modern goalkeeper role with expert position-specific drills.

  • Reaction & Agility Drills
  • Communication Mastery
  • Hand-Eye Coordination
  • Shot-Stopping Experts
