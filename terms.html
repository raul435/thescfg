Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: June 2025

1. Membership

Participation in THE SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA FOOTBALLING GROUP is voluntary and subject to acceptance by club management. The club reserves the right to suspend or remove any participant whose behavior negatively impacts the club, its players, staff, or reputation.

2. Fees and Payments

All fees must be paid by the stated due date unless otherwise agreed in writing. Fees are generally non-refundable except in cases approved by club management.

3. Player Conduct

All players are expected to:

  • Show respect to teammates, coaches, opponents, officials, and spectators.
  • Follow instructions from club staff.
  • Refrain from abusive language, bullying, discrimination, or misconduct.
  • Represent the club in a positive manner both in person and online.

4. Attendance

Players should attend scheduled training sessions and matches whenever possible. If a player cannot attend, they should notify a coach or club representative as soon as practical.

5. Health and Safety

Players and parents/guardians are responsible for informing the club of any relevant medical conditions, injuries, or allergies. Participation is at the player’s own risk, and appropriate safety equipment must be worn when required.

6. Photography and Media

The club may take photographs and videos during training sessions, matches, and events for promotional, educational, and social media purposes unless written notice is provided requesting otherwise.

7. Liability

THE SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA FOOTBALLING GROUP is not responsible for loss, theft, or damage to personal property. Participation in football activities involves inherent risks, and players participate at their own risk to the extent permitted by law.

8. Cancellations and Schedule Changes

Training sessions, matches, and events may be modified, postponed, or cancelled due to weather, facility availability, safety concerns, or other circumstances beyond the club’s control.

9. Agreement

By registering with or participating in THE SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA FOOTBALLING GROUP activities, players and/or their parents or guardians acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to these Terms & Conditions.
