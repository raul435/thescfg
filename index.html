Meet the Owner

Our owner is dedicated to the growth of football in Southern California. With years of experience as a player and coach along with a passion for the grassroots level, TSCFG is driven for the community and by the with a vision of Worldwide success.

"Football is more than just a game; it's a tool for character building and community strength. At TSCFG, we strive to provide the best environment for our players to thrive both on and off the pitch."
